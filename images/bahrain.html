About
An upcoming urban community that houses Bahrain Harbours iconic residential projects, The Harbour Row, Harbour Heights, Harbour Views, and Charthouse. Bahrain Harbour presents a unique blend of phenomenal architecture, world-class hotels, international dining experiences, cutting-edge entertainment, and a seaside marina promenade with exceptional views. In Bahrain Harbour, we give you a comprehensive lifestyle that’s lined with luxury. Bahrain Harbour spans over an area of land in the hearts of Bahrain and is within proximity to the landmarks in leisure, business, and transportation centers in the Kingdom of Bahrain.
Duration: 2-3 hours

About Us

ECOTEC Investment Group develops and finances the ECOTEC Group’s industrial, real estate, and strategic projects.

The ECOTEC Group operates across multiple sectors in eight Middle Eastern countries.

ECOTEC Investment Group is a family‑owned regional investment firm. Since 1994, we have developed and financed industrial projects, co‑invested with international manufacturers, and structured foreign direct investment into Egypt and the wider Middle East. We are headquartered in Cairo with an office in Riyadh.

Founded by Osama Amin, our team brings three decades of operating experience and relationships in high‑growth markets. We combine data‑driven analysis with hands‑on support for our portfolio companies.

Over this period we have developed long‑standing relationships with customers and public institutions across the Middle East—including ministries, regulators, state‑owned enterprises, and municipalities. We understand local procurement cycles and tendering, and we navigate permits, certifications, customs, and standards with precision. This practical access and regulatory knowledge help de‑risk market entry, compress approval timelines, and deliver compliant in‑country solutions.

We invest across education, healthcare, manufacturing, defense, real estate, and technology. Our focus is on technology transfer, building local capability, and creating durable value. We acquire and expand operating businesses and form joint ventures to localize manufacturing and service capability. We manage end‑to‑end regulatory execution—permits, certifications, and local‑content compliance—and deploy patient capital to partner across the full investment cycle.

Our approach is regional, entrepreneurial, and mission‑focused. We aim to create value for businesses and the communities they serve.

ECOTEC Investment Group is part of the ECOTEC Group, established in Egypt in 1994, with a presence across eight Middle Eastern countries and long‑term partnerships with international industrial companies such as Bandera, Bettini, GFM, and Navantia.

Investment Sectors

We deploy patient capital into real‑economy platforms where our regulatory access, local delivery, and partner ecosystem create durable advantage.

Plastics

Plastics, Packaging & Circular Economy

Advanced plastic manufacturing and sustainable packaging solutions

We develop and scale plastics and packaging platforms: high‑throughput film and extrusion lines, local compounding, and closed‑loop recycling. Turnkey OEM integration, localization, and lifecycle service.

Food & Agri

Food & Beverage Processing

Modern food processing and agricultural business solutions

We build modern processing and cold‑chain capacity from farm to fork: slaughtering, dairy, dates, beverages, and automated packaging with HACCP/ISO‑aligned quality systems.

Healthcare

Pharmaceuticals & Medical Devices

Advanced healthcare manufacturing and medical technology

We enable GMP‑compliant pharmaceutical and medical device manufacturing with technology transfer, cleanroom fit‑out, validation, and regulatory certification.

Textiles

Textiles and Nonwovens

Modern textile manufacturing and nonwoven fabric solutions

We expand textiles and nonwovens: spunbond/spunlace lines, technical fabrics, and backward integration. Local sourcing, energy optimization, and export‑grade QA.

Infrastructure

Construction & Infrastructure

Automated construction solutions and infrastructure development

Education & Real Estate
